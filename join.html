BECOME A MEMBER

Fight Club currently has over 2,000 affiliated men; the majority of which attend meetings regularly. We are always seeking new members of the club. If you’re ready to improve your physical and mental state, to push yourself to become better, stronger, and more resilient in every aspect, locate the nearest and soonest chapter meeting here and join us now!

YOU WILL GAIN...

  • Improved mental state
  • Physical betterment
  • Aptitude for discipline
  • Respect for oneself and others
  • Appreciation for struggle and loss
  • Acceptance of non-control
  • Mental, physical, and emotional strength
  • Invaluable friendships
  • Stable foundation of confidence
  • Grit and resilience
  • Purpose

HOW TO JOIN

It’s simple. Just find a chapter near you and go to the soonest meeting you can attend. All meetings gather in allowing bar basements. Sign in at the door when you get there, and most importantly, be ready to fight. All first time attendants must fight at their first meeting. To loacte a chapter near you, visit this page.
